AVO VIIRS Processing

Plots here are intended to show the health of VIIRS image processing. Each panel describes events at a different system boundary and attempts to provide insight into how well the system is behaving at that point. This is done by comparing current performance with the average performance over a measurement window. Along with the individual plots and tables, a single status indicator is displayed in each pace indicating overall health.
  • A green star means everything is good.
  • A yellow triangle means things seem worse than normal, but not completly broken.
  • A red circle means things seem clearly broken.
